Information We Collect

When you request a quote, we may collect the details you choose to submit, such as your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, preferred contact method, service type, home details, scheduling notes and selected add-on services.

We may also collect basic website activity information, such as page visits, form progress events and cookie preferences, to understand how the website is being used.

How We Use Information

DNA Cleaning Services uses submitted information to respond to quote requests, understand the cleaning service needed, communicate with potential clients, improve the website experience and maintain basic business records.

Form details are used to follow up about cleaning services. They are not sold as marketing lists.

How Information Is Shared

Information may be processed by trusted service providers that help operate the website, deliver form notifications, manage email communication, provide analytics or support business operations.

DNA Cleaning Services may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or to respond to lawful requests.

Cookies And Analytics

This website uses necessary browser storage to remember cookie preferences and may use optional analytics tools to understand site activity. Analytics events may include actions such as opening the quote form, reaching step two and submitting a request.

These analytics events are intended to measure website performance and do not need to include sensitive message content.

Your Choices

You can choose not to submit a quote form. You can also decline optional analytics cookies through the cookie banner. Browser settings may allow you to delete cookies or local storage for this website at any time.

To ask about your submitted information, contact DNA Cleaning Services using the phone number below.

Data Security

Reasonable steps are used to protect submitted information. No website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so only submit information you are comfortable sharing for a quote request.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults requesting cleaning services. It is not directed to children under 13, and DNA Cleaning Services does not knowingly collect information from children.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as the website, services or business processes change. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.
