What Cookies Are

Cookies and similar browser storage help a website remember small pieces of information. On this website, storage is used to remember your cookie preference and may support analytics if you accept optional analytics.

Cookies We Use

DNA Cleaning Services keeps cookie use limited and practical.

Necessary storage

Used to remember your cookie preference and keep the website experience consistent. This cannot be turned off from the banner because it supports the preference itself.

Optional analytics

Used only if accepted, to understand website activity and improve the quote process, content and conversion experience.

Analytics Events

The website may send measurement events such as opening the quote form, reaching step two of the form, submitting a quote request or updating cookie consent. These events help evaluate whether the website experience is working clearly.

Analytics events should measure behavior, not expose private form messages.

Managing Cookie Choices

You can choose "Necessary only" or "Accept analytics" through the cookie banner. If you want to reset your choice later, clear this website's cookies and local storage in your browser settings, then reload the site to see the banner again.
